# Reach Enchantment

Reach is a new enchantment for mining tools and weapons with three levels that increases your block and entity interaction range.

The enchantment can be obtained from the enchantment table, loot chests or trading with villagers.

## ⌨️ Commands

Give:

```mcfunction
/loot give @s loot reach_enchantment:<level>
```

Enchant:

```mcfunction
/enchant @s reach_enchantment:reach <level>
```
